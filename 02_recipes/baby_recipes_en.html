Baby Recipes for 13-Month-Old

Balanced recipes following Japanese nutrient guidelines for a 13-month-old baby.

1. Soft Rice and Vegetable Mix

Ingredients:
  • Softly cooked rice 50g
  • Grated carrot 10g
  • Finely chopped spinach 10g
  • Dashi broth (or water) 1 tbsp
Instructions:
  1. Boil carrot and spinach until soft.
  2. Mix with rice and add dashi broth for extra softness.
  3. Cool to room temperature before serving.

Rich in iron and vitamins.

2. Sweet Potato and White Fish Stew

Ingredients:
  • Sweet potato (peeled, small cubes) 30g
  • White fish (cod, sea bream) 20g
  • Dashi broth 100ml
Instructions:
  1. Boil sweet potato until soft.
  2. Flake the fish and simmer in dashi broth.
  3. Mix with sweet potato and cool before serving.

Good source of protein and fiber.

3. Banana and Yogurt Dessert

Ingredients:
  • Ripe banana 20g
  • Plain yogurt 1 tbsp
Instructions:
  1. Mash the banana with a fork.
  2. Mix with yogurt.
  3. Chill and serve.

Rich in calcium and potassium.

These recipes are designed to meet the nutritional needs of a 13-month-old baby. Adjust according to allergies or preferences.
